Cookies Policy

Last updated: March 2025

This policy explains how cookies and similar technologies are used on neozetti.com.ar.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember preferences, improve navigation and deliver personalized content.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential: needed for basic site functions like remembering language selection.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics cookies help understand how visitors use the site.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense cookies personalize ads and measure their performance.

Managing cookies

  • You can clear or block cookies from your browser settings.
  • You may manage Google ad personalization at Ad Settings.
  • Disabling cookies may affect some site features.

Third-party cookies

AdSense and Analytics place their own cookies. Please review their policies for details on data handling.

For more information, read our Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions.
