About this site

Hi! I am Nazareno Ordiz (Neozetti), a game developer and player from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I built this site to share devlogs, reviews and anything that's on my mind.

My goal is to publish original articles, to show how is to be an indie dev. Also, I want to share some words related to the games that I'm playing.

Who is behind Neozetti?

  • Lives and operates from Buenos Aires, Argentina.
