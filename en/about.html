About this site

Hi! I am Nazareno Ordiz (Neozetti), a game developer and writer from Buenos Aires, Argentina. I built this bilingual site to share devlogs, reviews and design notes for the projects I am creating.

My goal is to publish original articles that help players and fellow indie developers. Every post is written or translated by me to keep both English and Spanish sections consistent.

Who is behind Neozetti?

  • Independent creator working on narrative-driven games.
  • Publishes development updates, reviews and resources with clear sources.
  • Lives and operates from Buenos Aires, Argentina.

How to contact me

The easiest way to reach out is by email at neozetti@gmail.com. You can also find me on Twitter, Bluesky, TikTok or Discord.
